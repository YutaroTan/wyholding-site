Maritime Industry Chain

Global Full-Spectrum Shipping and Maritime Services

W.Y. (Europe) Holding GmbH, in collaboration with members of the China Maritime Alliance (a non-profit maritime charity network of over 270 companies and individuals), Seawave Marine, and Eureka (Germany), provides end-to-end maritime industry chain services, including:

  • Ship financing
  • Ship design
  • Construction and supervision
  • Repair and retrofitting
  • Ships Chartering
  • Financing and Leasing
  • Sales and Purchase
  • Scraping dismantling

Together, we are building a globally connected platform for comprehensive shipping and maritime services.Leveraging Eureka Germany's shareholder strengths in European food production and distribution, international park development, trade, and ship supply chains, the joint venture provides integrated services centered in Hamburg and major European ports, including:

  • Ship provisions and bonded supply (duty-free tobacco & spirits, food supplies, technical items).
  • A one-stop global supply, trade, and logistics platform.
  • Strategic collaboration with the China Maritime Alliance to connect key global ports such as China, Singapore, and Dubai.

Additionally, we are establishing:

  • A ship supply overseas warehouse hub and manufacturing base in Croatia, Connect to major European ports overseas warehouse.
